Data DWP Authenticate will pass to your service

When a user successfully authenticates, DWP Authenticate will pass the following data about them to your service:

  • first name
  • last name
  • email address
  • organisation name

Additional data

You can define additional data about your users that DWP Authenticate can capture and send. You can use this feature to define specific user roles or teams you may have within your service.
